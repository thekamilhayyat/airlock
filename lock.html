Install Lock And Hub

AirLocking sends you a smart lock system. You just need to connect the Hub to the internet and follow the straightforward instructions to install the lock.

Link With Airbnb Calendar

AirLocking sends you a smart lock system. You just need to connect the Hub to the internet and follow the straightforward instructions to install the lock.

03

Guests Receive Door Codes

AirLocking automatically emails your guests door codes in advance of their trip along with other check-in information you wish to pass along. You are copied on these emails for your tracking.

04

Lock Updated With Codes

AirLocking enables the door code in the lock at check-in time and disables it after checkout time. AirLocking also allows you to have codes for your own use which are separate from the guest codes.
